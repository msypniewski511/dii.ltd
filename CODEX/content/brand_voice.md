# Brand Voice

## Current Voice
The homepage now leans toward:
- B2B parent-brand/product language
- strategic ecosystem messaging
- clearer visitor-facing explanations of where to start and what each layer does

## Desired Voice
- Clear
- practical
- structured
- credible
- concrete
- product-aware

## What The Voice Should Emphasize
- DII helps founders and operators move from confusion to structure.
- The company builds connected systems, not disconnected apps.
- Benefits should be stated in plain business language.
- The site should feel like a live company homepage, not a planning document.

## What To Avoid
- Talking to the site owner in live copy
- "This section can become" framing on production pages
- vague "innovation" language with no concrete outcome
- internal TODO notes visible to visitors
