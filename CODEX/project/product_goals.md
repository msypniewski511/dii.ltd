# Product Goals

## Primary Homepage Jobs
- Explain what DII is without making visitors infer the relationship between the products.
- Route visitors to the right entry point quickly.
- Make the parent company feel credible, structured, and product-led.
- Create a base that can later support a content and SEO engine.

## Current Implied Audiences
- Founders validating or structuring a business idea
- Founders exploring an Innovator Founder Visa path
- Operations teams needing rota planning or warehouse control
- Partners or buyers evaluating DII as a broader software group

## Desired Outcomes
- A visitor understands DII within the hero and first two sections.
- Product cards make the next click obvious.
- The page feels live and customer-facing, not like an internal concept deck.
- Future content expansion fits the existing structure without a rebuild.
