# Audience Personas

## Founder Explorer
- Needs: idea validation, planning structure, clearer next steps
- Likely destination: Startup Business Builder
- Relevant sections: hero, platform, products, how-it-works

## Visa-Path Founder
- Needs: evidence logic, founder readiness, endorsement-path guidance
- Likely destination: IFV Builder
- Relevant sections: hero carousel, products, contact

## Operations Lead
- Needs: rota planning, workforce coordination, warehouse visibility
- Likely destination: Rotaplan or Warewise
- Relevant sections: platform, products, how-it-works

## Partner Or Buyer Evaluator
- Needs: confidence that DII is a serious umbrella brand with organized contact paths
- Likely destination: contact section or direct outreach
- Relevant sections: hero, principles, contact
